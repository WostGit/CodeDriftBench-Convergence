# CodeDriftBench Claim Card

## Cite-safe claims

1. The benchmark separates functional tests from active durable specification constraints.
2. The evidence report includes real model outputs, multiple task families, context-strategy ablations, bootstrap confidence intervals, and publishability gates.

## Claims requiring observed effects

Do not claim that tests overestimate reliability unless `mean_test_spec_gap` is positive and practically meaningful. Do not claim convergence with scale unless model-order slopes are positive across horizons/tasks.
